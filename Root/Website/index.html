What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures, weather patterns, and climatic conditions across the globe. While some changes occur naturally over centuries or millennia, the rapid pace of recent changes is largely driven by human activities, such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes.

Impacts include rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and loss of biodiversity.
